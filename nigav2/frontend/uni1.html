About Himalayan Global University

HGU is a private, industry-focused university committed to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. We combine rigorous academics with real-world experiences — internships, live projects and campus placements — to prepare students for global careers.

  • Established: 2010
  • Accreditations: NAAC A+, AICTE approved

Undergraduate & Postgraduate programs across Engineering, Management and Applied Sciences

MBA — Marketing / Finance
